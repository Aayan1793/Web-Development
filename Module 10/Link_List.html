key terms

climate change
The long-term alteration of temperature and weather patterns, primarily due to human activities.
renewable energy
Energy derived from natural sources that are replenished at a higher rate than they are consumed.
biodiversity
The variety of life in a particular ecosystem or on the entire planet.
